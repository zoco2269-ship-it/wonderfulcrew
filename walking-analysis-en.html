Walking & Posture AI Analysis

Airlines evaluate walking posture and leg alignment carefully. Our AI analyzes your walking video and provides improvement points.

Recording Guide

  • Full body visible (head to toe)
  • Front + side views recommended
  • Record in a well-lit area (natural light recommended)
  • Wear comfortable dress shoes (same as interview)
  • 5-10 second walking video
Note: Straight leg line is preferred. Airlines especially evaluate walking posture and leg alignment.
