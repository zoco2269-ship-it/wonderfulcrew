flydubai Curriculum

flydubai
Complete Interview Prep

Dubai-based. Prepare for the cabin crew interview at Emirates' sister carrier, flydubai.

Online Application→ Assessment Day→ Final Interview→ Medical Check

Stage 1: Online Application

CV · Photo · Eligibility
  • Apply online via the flydubai careers site
  • English-language CV + full-body / head-and-shoulders photos
  • Minimum height: women 160cm / men 170cm
  • Arm Reach 212cm
  • Fluent English required
TIP: flydubai is a separate, independent airline from Emirates. Although it's an LCC, it operates a global network from its Dubai base, serving more than 100 cities.

Stage 2: Assessment Day

Group activities · English test · Arm Reach
  • On-site document check and image assessment
  • Arm Reach test
  • English test (written + speaking)
  • Group discussion: team task
  • Roleplay: in-flight service scenario simulation
TIP: flydubai has a more casual atmosphere than Emirates, but service mindset and teamwork matter just as much.

Stage 3: Final Interview

1:1 interview · in-depth questions
  • 1:1 individual interview
  • Why flydubai?
  • In-depth service experience questions
  • Willingness to adapt to life in Dubai
  • Understanding of how flydubai differs from Emirates
TIP: "I want to grow alongside an expanding airline" is an effective message. Know flydubai's unique angle (LCC + global network).

Stage 4: Medical Check · Background Check

Physical exam · criminal record check
  • Medical check at a designated hospital
  • Criminal record check
  • Dubai visa issuance procedures
TIP: A medical check and background screening are conducted as part of the Dubai visa process.
