International Airline Interview Guide

Emirates
UAE · Dubai
Emirates Airline
Interview Process (7 Stages)
  1. Online Application / CV Drop Submit your CV. Receive an invitation to an Open Day or Assessment Day.
  2. Small Talk A 1:1 casual conversation with the recruiter, evaluating first impression and English communication.
  3. First Round Presentation A one-minute English presentation in front of the group on a given topic.
  4. Group Discussion Team-based debate that assesses contribution, active listening, and leadership.
  5. English Test Reading comprehension, parts of speech, and vocabulary assessment.
  6. Roleplay In-flight scenario simulation that evaluates service mindset and problem-solving.
  7. Final Interview 1:1 in-depth interview covering values, service experience, and motivation for joining Emirates.
TIP: Emirates values 'Ownership' and 'Going Beyond'. Show a mindset that creates memorable moments for passengers, rather than delivering routine service. Your English does not have to be flawless what matters is speaking with clarity and confidence using simple, natural language.

Emirates Past Questions

[ Small Talk / CV Drop ]

  1. Please introduce yourself. (Self-introduction)
  2. Why do you want to be cabin crew? (Motivation)
  3. What do you do currently? (Current role)

[ First Round Presentation / Discussion ]

  1. What is your favourite travel destination and why? (Favourite destination)
  2. If you could sit next to a celebrity, who and why? (Celebrity seatmate)
  3. Describe your best customer service experience. (Best service experience)

[ Group Discussion ]

  1. Group discussion: Prioritize cabin crew duties in order of importance (Priority debate)
  2. Overbooking scenario: 7 passengers, 1 extra legroom seat (Overbooking scenario)
  3. Hotel manager scenario: 10 guests, 2 rooms available (Hotel manager scenario)

[ Roleplay ]

  1. What would you do if a passenger refused to wear a seatbelt? (Seatbelt refusal)
  2. A passenger's meal choice has run out. How do you handle it? (Meal sold out)
  3. How would you handle a language barrier? (Language barrier)

[ Final Interview ]

  1. Tell me about your best service experience. (Best service experience)
  2. Have you ever dealt with a demanding customer? (Demanding customer)
  3. Tell me about a time you helped a colleague. (Helping a colleague)
  4. Have you ever had a conflict with a colleague? (Colleague conflict)
  5. Have you ever shown respect for Muslim culture? (Respect for Muslim culture)
  6. Why do you want to work for Emirates? (Motivation for Emirates)
Qatar Airways
Qatar · Doha
Qatar Airways
Interview Process
  1. Online Application → CV Screening You must receive an invitation to proceed to the next stage.
  2. Assessment Day Group discussion + English test + a short presentation.
  3. Final Interview 1:1 in-depth interview covering service mindset, values, and teamwork.
  4. Medical Check → Final Offer
TIP: Qatar Airways takes great pride in its '5-star service'. Highlight an attentive, refined, and luxury-oriented service mindset. Note that Qatar's scar (visible mark) policy tends to be stricter than Emirates'.

Qatar Airways Past Questions

[ Assessment Day Group ]

  1. Tell me about yourself.
  2. Describe a time you provided excellent customer service.
  3. What do you know about Doha and Qatar?
  4. Group discussion: What qualities make a great cabin crew?

[ Final Interview ]

  1. Why Qatar Airways specifically?
  2. How do you handle stress?
  3. What would you do if a VIP passenger complained about the food?
  4. How do you work in a multicultural team?
  5. Are you willing to relocate to Doha?
  6. Tell me about a time you showed leadership.
  7. What makes you different from other candidates?
  8. A passenger is being verbally abusive to you. How do you respond?
Singapore Airlines
Singapore
Singapore Airlines
Interview Process
  1. Online Application Submit your photo, CV, and education details.
  2. Recruitment Briefing + Small Talk Basic English conversation and first impression assessment.
  3. Group Discussion Topic-based debate evaluating English expression and teamwork.
  4. English Test + Psychometric Assessment
  5. Final Interview 1:1 in-depth interview covering service philosophy, crisis response, and personal values.
  6. Water Survival Test + Medical Check
TIP: Singapore Airlines is renowned for the 'Singapore Girl' image. Display a service attitude that is both refined and genuinely warm. In the group discussion, it is essential to share your views while showing consideration for other candidates.

Singapore Airlines Past Questions

[ Recruitment Briefing + Small Talk ]

  1. Introduce yourself in 1 minute.
  2. Why cabin crew?
  3. What do you know about Singapore Airlines?

[ Group Discussion ]

  1. What qualities make the best cabin crew member?
  2. How would you handle a difficult passenger?
  3. Tell me about a time you worked as part of a team.

[ Final Interview ]

  1. Why do you want to join Singapore Airlines?
  2. What is your greatest strength and weakness?
  3. What does the Singapore Girl image mean to you?
  4. How do you stay calm under pressure?
  5. Are you comfortable living away from home in Singapore?
  6. Describe your ideal work environment.
  7. How would you handle a passenger who is afraid of turbulence?
Cathay Pacific
Hong Kong
Cathay Pacific
Interview Process
  1. Online Application CV and cover letter.
  2. Video Interview Pre-recorded format. Answer given questions in English on camera.
  3. Group Assessment Discussion + Roleplay + English test.
  4. Final Interview 1:1 in-depth interview.
  5. Medical Check → Final Offer
TIP: Cathay Pacific may include a Safety Demo during the interview. Familiarise yourself with safety equipment in advance and demonstrate it with confidence. A positive attitude toward life based in Hong Kong is equally important.

Cathay Pacific Past Questions

[ Video Interview ]

  1. Tell me about yourself.
  2. Why do you want to work for Cathay Pacific?
  3. What does good customer service mean to you?

[ Group Assessment ]

  1. What do you know about Hong Kong?
  2. Roleplay: A passenger is afraid of flying.
  3. Safety demo demonstration
  4. Group discussion: What makes a great team?

[ Final Interview ]

  1. Tell me about a time you made a mistake and fixed it.
  2. What would you do if a colleague wasn't following safety procedures?
  3. How do you build rapport with people from different cultures?
  4. How do you handle working irregular hours?
  5. What are your hobbies and how do they relate to this role?
  6. Why should we choose you?

AirAsia
Malaysia · Kuala Lumpur
AirAsia
Interview Process
  1. Online Application Submit your CV and photo. Walk-in recruitment is also held.
  2. Group Discussion English self-introduction + group debate. Energy and a bright personality are valued.
  3. 1:1 Interview Questions on service mindset, teamwork, and situational response.
  4. Swimming Test + Medical Check
  5. Final Offer
TIP: True to its low-cost identity, AirAsia favours bright, high-energy candidates. Aim for "fun, friendly, fearless" rather than formality. Cross-cultural teamwork experience is a major plus.

AirAsia Past Questions

[ Group Discussion ]

  1. Introduce yourself in 30 seconds.
  2. What makes a good team player?
  3. If you could change one thing about air travel, what would it be?

[ 1:1 Interview ]

  1. Why AirAsia?
  2. What does "Now Everyone Can Fly" mean to you?
  3. How would you handle an angry passenger on a budget airline?
  4. Tell me about a time you worked with people from different cultures.
  5. Are you comfortable with the base in Kuala Lumpur?
  6. What would you do if a colleague was not pulling their weight?
  7. Why should we pick you over other candidates?

KLM Royal Dutch Airlines
Netherlands · Amsterdam
KLM Royal Dutch Airlines
Interview Process
  1. Online Application Submit your CV and motivation letter.
  2. Online Assessment Personality assessment + situational judgement test.
  3. Video Interview Pre-recorded English Q&A.
  4. Assessment Day (Amsterdam) Group activities + Roleplay + 1:1 interview + swimming test.
  5. Medical Check → Final Offer
TIP: KLM is part of the Air France-KLM Group, one of Europe's largest airline groups, and places strong emphasis on diversity and inclusion. Showcase your cross-cultural experience and a flexible, open mindset.

KLM Past Questions

[ Video Interview ]

  1. Why do you want to work for KLM?
  2. Tell us about a challenge you overcame.
  3. What does diversity mean to you?

[ Assessment Day Group Activities ]

  1. Group task: Plan a team-building event with a limited budget.
  2. Roleplay: A passenger missed their connecting flight due to a delay.
  3. Roleplay: A passenger has a severe nut allergy and is worried about the meal.

[ 1:1 Interview ]

  1. Tell me about yourself.
  2. What do you know about the Netherlands and Amsterdam?
  3. How do you work in a multicultural environment?
  4. Describe a time you had to deal with a difficult colleague.
  5. How would you handle a medical emergency on board?
  6. What makes KLM different from other airlines?
  7. Are you prepared to relocate to Amsterdam?
