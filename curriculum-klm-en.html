KLM Curriculum

KLM Royal Dutch Airlines
Interview Preparation

Based in the Netherlands. Prepare for every stage of the KLM cabin crew interview process.

Online Application→ Digital Tests→ Meet & Greet→ Group Assignment→ CBI Interview

Stage 1: Online Application

CV · Photos · Language Proof
  • Complete the online application on the KLM Careers portal
  • Upload your CV/resume
  • Attach a recent photograph
  • Language proof: English (advanced level required)
  • Visa / work-eligibility proof
TIP: Verify your English-language proof and visa/work eligibility carefully before applying.

Stage 2: Digital Tests

English · Numerical · Competency Assessment
  • English proficiency test
  • Numerical reasoning test
  • Competency-based assessment
TIP: Brush up on your English fundamentals and basic numerical reasoning. Time management is critical.

Stage 3: Meet & Greet

Self-Introduction · Conversation with Recruiters
  • Brief self-introduction in Dutch
  • Natural conversation with recruiters
  • Assessment of first impression and communication
TIP: KLM requires Dutch language skills. A natural, friendly conversation is what matters most.

Stage 4: Group Assignment

Team Task · Roleplay · Problem Solving
  • Team assignment: collaborate to achieve a specific objective
  • Roleplay: in-flight service scenario simulation
  • Problem-solving exercise
  • Assessment of teamwork and cooperation
TIP: In the group assignment, show cooperation over leadership. Active listening and respect are key.

Stage 5: CBI Interview (Criteria-Based Interview)

Competency-Based Interview · 2 Interviewers
  • Competency-based interview with two interviewers
  • Specific examples from service experience
  • Assessment of teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving
  • Why KLM?
  • Questions on adaptability to life abroad
  • What do you think of the direct Dutch communication style?
TIP: The CBI follows a "Tell me about a time when..." format. Use the STAR method (Situation–Task–Action–Result) and prepare concrete examples.
