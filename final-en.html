The Final Interview is a deep 1:1 or 1:2 behavioral interview. At this stage the interviewer makes a holistic judgement: "Is this person really the right fit for our airline?" Both answer structure and authenticity are weighed.

Top 5 Questions That Always Come Up

  1. Tell me about yourself — One-minute self-introduction
  2. Why this airline? — Motivation for the company
  3. Why cabin crew? — Motivation for the profession
  4. Tell me about a difficult customer experience — STAR method
  5. Why should we hire you? — Strengths pitch

STAR Method (structure for difficult-experience answers)

  • Situation — what the situation was
  • Task — your role in it
  • Action — what you specifically did
  • Result — outcome plus what you learned

"Why this airline?" Pass-Level Answer Structure

① One distinctive value of the airline (Emirates: Diversity / Qatar: Service Excellence / Singapore: Singapore Girl Heritage)

② Connect it to your own experience

③ Your future vision

Answers to Absolutely Avoid

  • "I love traveling" — too common (100 other candidates will give the same answer)
  • "It's my dream" — too abstract
  • Disparaging comparisons of other airlines
