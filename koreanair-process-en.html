2
1st Practical Interview (Group of 5-6 · in Korean and English)

6

Korean Air recruitment runs: document screen → video interview (newly introduced in 2025) → practical interview (groups of 8) → executive interview → fitness test and medical including a 25m swim. Self-introduction and video interview preparation are the key focus areas.
