Cathay Pacific Curriculum

Cathay Pacific
Complete Interview Prep

Hong Kong-based. Master every stage of the Cathay Pacific cabin crew interview.

Online Application→ Online Assessment→ Assessment Day→ Individual Interview

Stage 1: Online Application

Resume · Eligibility
  • Apply online via the Cathay Pacific careers site
  • Upload an English-language resume
  • Evidence of English proficiency
TIP: Cathay Pacific is Hong Kong-based. Highlight overseas experience and multicultural adaptability in your CV.

Stage 2: Online Assessment · Digital Interview

SJT · English test · personality profiling · video interview
  • Situational Judgement Test (SJT)
  • English reading comprehension test
  • Personality profiling
  • Digital interview: 3 questions (1 min prep + 2 min answer)
  • Why Cathay Pacific? Service-experience questions (in English)
TIP: The digital interview is taken from home. Stay concise — keep only the essence. For the SJT, choose answers that demonstrate a service mindset.

Stage 3: Assessment Day

Group activities · Arm Reach · service scenarios
  • Held at Cathay City (Hong Kong) or an overseas hiring city
  • Arm Reach test: 208cm or above
  • Group activity: passenger service scenario task
  • Teamwork, communication and professionalism are assessed
  • Safety Demo demonstration possible
TIP: Assessment Day runs all day. Manage your stamina and keep your energy bright. You're being assessed even while waiting.

Stage 4: Individual Interview

Competency-based interview · 20–40 minutes
  • 1:1 interview (20–40 minutes)
  • Competency-based questions: "Tell me about a time when..."
  • Motivation and long-term plan
  • Concrete examples of service experience
  • Willingness to adapt to life in Hong Kong
  • Possible English / Mandarin assessment
TIP: Answer using the STAR method. Concrete experience + concrete result. Mandarin proficiency is a major plus.
