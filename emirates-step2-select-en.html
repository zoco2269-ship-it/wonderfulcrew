Video Interview & Small Talk

Choose which of the two assessment formats to practice now.
Online applicants face the Video Interview; Open Day candidates begin with CV Small Talk — we recommend rehearsing both.

🎬
Video Interview

Self-Recorded Video Interview

The self-recorded interview used for online applications. You hear a question on camera and record your answer in English.

  • One question at a time, randomly drawn
  • Camera recording with voice recognition
  • Re-record or move to the next question
  • Evaluated on first impression, smile and confidence

CV Small Talk

CV Drop Small Talk

A short 1:1 English exchange with the recruiter at the Open Day. A natural ping-pong of greeting, motivation and follow-up questions.

  • You open with the greeting
  • AI interviewer replies briefly and adds follow-ups
  • Evaluated on first impression and English flow
  • Ping-pong dialogue — you decide when to wrap up
