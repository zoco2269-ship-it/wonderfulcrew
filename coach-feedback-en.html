2026-04-09 · Emirates · Final Interview · Video
Why do you want to work for Emirates?
Coach Feedback:

The specific service examples in your answer stood out, and tying your hotel experience to in-flight service worked well.

A few points to sharpen:

1) Never say "I love travelling." Emirates interviewers have heard this thousands of times. Cabin crew is a profession, not a holiday. You start a shift in Seoul and open the cabin door in Paris — that is work, not a trip.

2) "I want to provide good service" is too abstract. Instead, describe an actual service moment you delivered. For example, "I read a customer's need before they asked and moved on it" lets the interviewer picture you in the cabin.

3) The closing was weak. End with a single line that ties your strength to an Emirates value: "I believe my ability to read people's needs and act before they ask makes me a natural fit for Emirates."

The direction is solid. Polish these points and this answer can carry you to the Final. You've got this.
