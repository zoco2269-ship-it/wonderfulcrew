Qatar Airways Curriculum

Qatar Airways
Interview Preparation

Based in Doha. Recruits worldwide through Open Days. Prepare for every stage of the Qatar Airways cabin crew interview.

Online Application→ CV Drop · Small Talk→ English Test · Physical Check→ Word Shooting→ Final Interview

Stage 1: Online Application

CV · Photo Upload
  • Apply online via the Qatar Airways Careers portal
  • Upload your English CV / resume
  • Attach a full-length photo and a headshot
TIP: Write your CV in English. Take your photos in a polished business look with a bright, natural smile.

Stage 2: CV Drop · Small Talk

First Impression · CV Submission · 1:1 Small Talk
  • Submit your CV and photo on site
  • First impression assessment: smile, posture, grooming
  • 1:1 small talk (brief English conversation)
  • Visible tattoo and scar check
TIP: CV Drop is the single most decisive stage in the Qatar Airways interview. First impression accounts for ninety percent — bright smile, immaculate business suit.

Stage 3: English Test · Physical Check

English / Math Written Test · Height & Arm Reach · Individual Presentation
  • Written English test plus a math test
  • Height check: minimum 157.5 cm / Arm Reach 212 cm or above (tiptoes allowed)
  • Individual presentation
TIP: This stage runs all day. The English test is time-tight — build up your speed beforehand with daily English shadowing.

Stage 4: Word Shooting · Sentence Shooting

Random Keywords · Spontaneous Presentation
  • Draw a random keyword and deliver a one-minute presentation on the spot
  • Sentence shooting: respond immediately to a given sentence
  • Assesses spoken English plus quick thinking
TIP: Not perfect English — confident delivery is the heart of it. Practice starting to speak the instant you receive the keyword.

Stage 5: Final Interview

1:1 Final Interview · In-Depth Questions · Compensation Briefing
  • One-on-one interview (approximately 20–30 minutes)
  • Why Qatar Airways?
  • Specific examples from service experience
  • Willingness to adapt to life in Doha
  • Questions on stamina for long-haul flights
  • Compensation, salary and benefits briefing
TIP: Authenticity decides the Final. Express a positive attitude toward life in Doha and adapting to cultural differences. Prepare your best stories in advance.
