✨ WonderfulCrew Audio Interview Tips for Foreign Applicants ▼ Tap to read

Korean Air is one of the most prestigious employers for foreign cabin crew, and the panel almost always wants to hear you speak Korean — even from international applicants. If you are stronger in Thai for now, practice in Thai first and migrate to Korean as your confidence grows.

How to Use This Audio Practice

  • Pick the language you plan to use in the real interview.
  • Listen carefully — the interviewer voice models the pace of a real Korean Air panel.
  • Speak naturally. Don't read from a script. The AI evaluates substance, not perfect grammar.
  • If you missed the question, tap Replay Question to hear it again.

What Korean Air Looks For

  • Warm, sincere energy — not over-rehearsed.
  • Clear motivation: Why Korean Air specifically, not just any airline.
  • A concrete service philosophy backed by your own experience.
  • Comfortable Korean (or Thai) listening — you should be able to react, not just deliver.
