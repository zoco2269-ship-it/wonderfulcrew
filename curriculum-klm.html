KLM Curriculum

KLM Royal Dutch Airlines
Complete Interview Prep

Netherlands-based. Master every stage of the KLM cabin crew interview.

Online Application→ Digital Tests→ Meet & Greet→ Group Assignment→ CBI Interview

Stage 1: Online Application

CV · Photo · Language proof
  • Submit your application via the KLM careers site
  • Upload your CV/resume
  • Attach a recent photo
  • Language proof: English at advanced level (required)
  • Visa / right-to-work documentation
TIP: Verify English proficiency proof and visa / right-to-work eligibility carefully in advance.

Stage 2: Digital Tests

English · Maths · competency assessment
  • English proficiency test
  • Maths test
  • Competency-based assessment
TIP: Refresh your English and basic maths. Time allocation matters.

Stage 3: Meet & Greet

Self-introduction · recruiter conversation
  • Short self-introduction in Dutch
  • Natural conversation with the recruiter
  • First impression and communication ability are assessed
TIP: KLM requires Dutch language proficiency. Aim for natural, warm conversation.

Stage 4: Group Assignment

Group task · roleplay · problem solving
  • Group task: a team task to achieve a specific goal
  • Roleplay: in-flight service scenario simulation
  • Problem-solving exercise
  • Teamwork and cooperation are assessed
TIP: In group tasks, show cooperation rather than leadership. Listening and respect are key.

Stage 5: CBI Interview (Criteria Based Interview)

Competency-based interview · 2 interviewers
  • Competency-based interview with 2 interviewers
  • Concrete-example questions about service experience
  • Teamwork, leadership, problem-solving competencies are assessed
  • Why KLM?
  • Questions on adaptability to life abroad
  • How do you feel about the Dutch culture of direct communication?
TIP: The CBI interview uses the "Tell me about a time when..." format. Prepare concrete examples using the STAR method (Situation–Task–Action–Result).
