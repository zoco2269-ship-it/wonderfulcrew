Other International Airlines
Interview Prep

Review each airline's interview characteristics below and prepare using the Emirates curriculum as your foundation.

Middle East

Kuwait Airways

Kuwait-based
  • Has hired directly from Korea (via CrewKorea)
  • Interview: CV submission → Assessment Day → final interview
  • Actively hires Korean cabin crew

Gulf Air

Bahrain-based
  • Global hiring, renewable contracts
  • Bahrain's national carrier

Oman Air

Oman · Muscat-based
  • Active international hiring
  • Female applicant age range 21–31

Saudia

Saudi Arabia · Jeddah / Riyadh-based
  • Saudi flag carrier, active international hiring
  • Middle East network + special Hajj / Umrah routes

Air Arabia

UAE · Sharjah-based
  • Middle East's largest LCC — 60+ A320s, 170+ routes
  • Global hiring (Open Days · online applications)
  • Arm Reach 212cm, English required
TIP: Like Emirates and flydubai, it's UAE-based, but operates from Sharjah. Hiring is fast-paced as is typical for an LCC.
Asia

Scoot

Singapore-based · Singapore Airlines subsidiary
  • SIA Group's LCC, active international hiring
  • Focused on medium / long-haul Asia–Australia routes
TIP: The curriculum and interview style are similar to Singapore Airlines. SIA prep is helpful here.

Vietnam Airlines

Vietnam · Hanoi / Ho Chi Minh-based
  • Has directly hired Korean cabin crew (via CrewKorea)
  • Vietnam's flag carrier, SkyTeam member

Thai Airways

Thailand · Bangkok-based
  • Thailand's flag carrier, Star Alliance member
  • Conducts international hiring

Starlux Airlines

Taiwan · Taipei-based
  • Premium new carrier launched in 2020
  • Growing rapidly, expanding North America routes

AirAsia X

Malaysia · Kuala Lumpur-based
  • AirAsia's medium / long-haul subsidiary
  • Separate hiring rounds for Korean / Japanese nationals
Europe

Turkish Airlines

Turkey · Istanbul-based
  • The airline serving the most countries in the world (120+)
  • Runs a large-scale international hiring programme
  • Interview: online application → CV Drop → Assessment Day → final interview
TIP: Turkish Airlines' biggest strength is its global network. Highlight your multicultural communication and adaptability.

British Airways

United Kingdom · Heathrow-based
  • International hiring via Talent Pool
  • UK work visa required
Americas

Delta Air Lines

USA · Atlanta-based
  • Actively hires Korean-speaking cabin crew (LOD programme)
  • Separate openings for Korean-speaking candidates
TIP: Delta runs separate hiring for Korean-speaking crew. Highlight Korea-route experience and bilingual ability.

Hawaiian Airlines

USA · Honolulu-based
  • Operates direct Incheon–Honolulu service; has hired Korean-speaking cabin crew
  • Merged with Alaska Airlines in 2024 — brand still operating separately
  • Pacific routes, Hawaii-based
TIP: Post-merger, hiring may be consolidated under Alaska Airlines — check both careers pages.
