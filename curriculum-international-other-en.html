Other International Carriers
Interview Preparation

Review the hiring characteristics of these airlines and build on the core Emirates curriculum to prepare for your interview.

Middle East

Kuwait Airways

Kuwait · Kuwait City Base
  • Has actively recruited from Korea (via CrewKorea)
  • Process: CV submission → Assessment Day → Final Interview
  • Strong record of hiring Korean cabin crew

Gulf Air

Bahrain · Manama Base
  • Global recruitment with renewable contracts
  • Bahrain's national carrier

Oman Air

Oman · Muscat Base
  • Active international recruitment
  • Female applicant age range: 21–31

Saudia

Saudi Arabia · Jeddah / Riyadh Base
  • Saudi national carrier with active international recruitment
  • Strong Middle East network plus special Hajj / Umrah routes

Air Arabia

UAE · Sharjah Base
  • Middle East's largest LCC — 60+ A320 aircraft, 170+ routes
  • Global recruitment (Open Days and online applications)
  • Arm Reach 212 cm; English required
TIP: Based in Sharjah (UAE), distinct from Emirates and flydubai. As an LCC, expect a fast recruitment process.
Asia

Scoot

Singapore · Singapore Base · Singapore Airlines Subsidiary
  • LCC arm of the Singapore Airlines Group with active international recruitment
  • Focuses on medium- to long-haul Asia–Australia routes
TIP: Curriculum and interview style closely mirror Singapore Airlines. Preparing for SIA interviews directly helps here.

Vietnam Airlines

Vietnam · Hanoi / Ho Chi Minh Base
  • Track record of directly hiring Korean cabin crew (via CrewKorea)
  • Vietnam's national carrier; SkyTeam member

Thai Airways

Thailand · Bangkok Base
  • Thailand's national carrier; Star Alliance member
  • Conducts international recruitment

Starlux Airlines

Taiwan · Taipei Base
  • Premium new carrier launched in 2020
  • Rapidly growing with expanding North American routes

AirAsia X

Malaysia · Kuala Lumpur Base
  • AirAsia's long-haul subsidiary
  • Separate recruitment for Korean and Japanese nationals
Europe

Turkish Airlines

Turkey · Istanbul Base
  • Operates to the most countries in the world (120+)
  • Runs large-scale international recruitment programs
  • Process: online application → CV Drop → Assessment Day → Final Interview
TIP: Turkish Airlines' global network is its greatest strength. Emphasize cross-cultural communication and adaptability.

British Airways

United Kingdom · Heathrow Base
  • Talent Pool style international recruitment
  • UK work visa required
Americas

Delta Air Lines

USA · Atlanta Base
  • Active recruitment of Korean-speaking cabin crew (LOD program)
  • Dedicated postings for Korean-speaking candidates
TIP: Delta runs a separate hiring track for Korean-speaking cabin crew. Highlight Korean-route experience and bilingual ability.

Hawaiian Airlines

USA · Honolulu Base
  • Operates Incheon–Honolulu direct flights with a history of Korean-speaking hires
  • Merged with Alaska Airlines in 2024 — branding remains separate for now
  • Pacific-focused network from a Hawaii base
TIP: Post-merger recruitment may consolidate into Alaska Airlines. Monitor both careers pages.

Beyond Emirates, Qatar, Singapore Airlines and Cathay — Etihad, flydubai, Finnair, KLM, Lufthansa, Riyadh Air, AirAsia and more — here are the shared hiring patterns to know.

Common Hiring Patterns Across Foreign Carriers

  • Open Day or CV Drop event → first-round cutoff
  • Natural conversational English is essential
  • Full grooming set (hair bun + makeup + suit)
  • Cross-cultural adaptability is assessed
