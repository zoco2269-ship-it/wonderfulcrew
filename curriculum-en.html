How Training Works

Step 01

Level Test

Quick diagnostic on your starting answer length, tone, and English fluency. Sets the tailored practice path.

Step 02

Roleplay & Discussion

Self-paced sessions for self-introduction, behavioral questions, group discussion, and pressure interviews. Instant scoring after each answer.

Step 03

Coach Feedback

Optional 1:1 review by the founding coach for high-stakes answers. Premium plan includes monthly live sessions and full lecture access.

Step 04

Final Mock & Apply

Full-format mock interview matching the target airline's actual stages, then apply with confidence at Open Day or online channels.
