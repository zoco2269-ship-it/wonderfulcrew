CV Drop small talk is the first 30-second English exchange between the interviewer and the candidate. Airline interviewers (Emirates, Qatar, Singapore Airlines) read your fluency, confidence and service mindset all at once in those 30 seconds. A natural first hello that does not sound rehearsed is the heart of it.

Top 5 Small Talk Topics Interviewers Love

  1. Weather and the venue — "Beautiful weather today, isn't it? How is the event going so far?"
  2. Where you are from — "I'm from Seoul, have you ever been there?"
  3. Interviewer's base city — "I heard Dubai is amazing this time of year."
  4. One-line career hook — "I've been working in customer service for three years and I love meeting people from different cultures."
  5. One-line motivation — "I've always admired Emirates' service standards."

Mistakes to Avoid

  • Scripts that sound memorized — interviewers detect this within half a second
  • Talking too long — keep impact within 30 seconds
  • Avoiding eye contact — eye contact is the measure of confidence
  • Cliché openers like "I want to be a flight attendant because..."

Sample Pass-Level Opening

"Good morning, my name is Kate, nice to meet you."

A short and confident greeting paired with handing over your CV — the interviewer naturally takes it from there. A short, sincere first hello wins more favor than a long rehearsed script.
